What ChemTrack Does

ChemTrack is your comprehensive solution for managing chemical inventories in laboratories, educational institutions, and industrial settings. We provide a robust platform to keep track of every chemical item, from its initial registration to its final usage.

  • Centralized Inventory: Keep all your chemical items organized in one accessible place.
  • Usage Tracking: Monitor how much of each chemical is used and by whom.
  • Low Stock Alerts: Receive notifications for chemicals running low, preventing unexpected shortages.
  • Reporting & Export: Generate detailed reports on inventory, usage, and stock levels.
  • User Management: Admins can manage users, roles, and access permissions.
